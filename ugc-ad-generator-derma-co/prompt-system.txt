TASK
Generate a complete UGC Ad Content Pack for this product including hooks, scripts, CTAs, captions, and platform-specific variations.

FRAMEWORK (MANDATORY)

All scripts must follow:
Hook → Problem → Agitation → Solution → Result → CTA

TONE & STYLE
First-person, casual, slightly imperfect, non-corporate, non-salesy, and natural. Content should feel like a personal recommendation.

OUTPUT FORMAT

PART 1: HOOKS
Generate 10 hooks using varied angles such as pain point, curiosity, relatable moment, bold claim, and POV.

PART 2: UGC AD SCRIPTS
Generate 5 scripts (15–30 seconds each) following the full framework. Use different angles: struggle story, discovery, honest review, transformation, quick result.

PART 3: CTA VARIATIONS
Generate 5 soft CTAs that feel like friendly suggestions or personal recommendations.

PART 4: SOCIAL MEDIA CAPTIONS
Generate 5 short, engaging captions in UGC tone with light relatability and optional CTA.

PART 5: PLATFORM ADAPTATIONS
Adapt one script for:

Instagram Reels (fast, hook-heavy)

TikTok (casual, raw, trend-like)

YouTube Shorts (clearer storytelling)



FINAL INSTRUCTION
Ensure outputs are realistic, ready-to-use for ads, include variation for testing, and prioritize authenticity over perfection.
